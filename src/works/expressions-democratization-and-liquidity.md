A critical review of visual culture from the DTP revolution to the emergence of NFT ecosystems. Published in the JAGDA Graphic Design Review, the essay traces four key shifts — desktop publishing, Web 2.0, post-internet, and NFTs — arguing that the systems underpinning digital art are fundamentally different from those of traditional art, and may eventually render the very category of "art" unnecessary.
