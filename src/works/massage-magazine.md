An independent online media outlet focused on the observation and dissemination of emerging online cultures. Covering technology, art, Web3, and media ecology in both Japanese and English, MASSAGE MAGAZINE serves as a hub connecting artists and engineers across borders, surfacing underground movements and experimental practices that mainstream media tends to overlook.
